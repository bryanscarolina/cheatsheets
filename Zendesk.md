## Zendesk Keyboard Shortcuts

### Tickets

**Action** | **Shortcut**
:-----:|:-----:
Open Macro Box | `^` + `⌥` + `M`
Save/Update as Open | `^` + `⌥` + `O`
Save/Update as Pending | `^` + `⌥` + `P`
Save/Update as On Hold | `^` + `⌥` + `D`
Save/Update as Solved | `^` + `⌥` + `S`
Move to Next Ticket | `^` + `⌥` + `↓`

### General Navigation

**Action** | **Shortcut**
:-----:|:-----:
Home | `^` + `⌥` + `H`
Views | `^` + `⌥` + `V`
Current Ticket | `^` + `⌥` + `T`
Search | `^` + `⌥` + `F`
New Ticket | `^` + `⌥` + `N`
Close Current Tab | `^` + `⌥` + `W`
